## 2026-07-06 Turkey River pass

- Added `turkey-river-gilbertson-big-spring`, `turkey-river-motor-mill-garber`, and `turkey-river-elkader-garber`.
- The implementation package relied on the 2025 Turkey River Water Trail guide PDF for the official access-chain mileage, Miles Paddled for exact route narratives and conservative threshold language, Fayette / Clayton county park pages for named public endpoints and camping context, and same-day USGS Water Services for live gauge confirmation.
- `turkey-river-gilbertson-big-spring` uses the Elkader gauge as a downstream same-river proxy with a conservative 250 cfs minimum-only floor tied to the Elgin-to-Big-Spring route report.
- `turkey-river-motor-mill-garber` and `turkey-river-elkader-garber` use the direct Garber gauge with a conservative 550 cfs minimum-only floor tied to the exact route day documented by Miles Paddled.
- Bounded image search stayed negative for all three new slugs. Existing Turkey River bridge/context imagery was not extended because no clearly rights-clean exact-route paddling photo surfaced for these reaches.
