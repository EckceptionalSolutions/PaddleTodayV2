# South Dakota Route Memory

Last summarized: 2026-05-31.

## Current Inventory

- Live routes: 3.
- Rivers represented: Big Sioux River, Skunk Creek, Split Rock Creek.
- Ledger candidates: 13 total; 3 added, 5 `research_later`, 2 `threshold_weak`, 2 `no_live_gauge`, 1 `gauge_proxy_weak`.

## Status

South Dakota has sparse coverage and a small candidate base. It is not exhausted, but it needs targeted source work before more routes are likely to clear the bar.

## Main Blockers

- Limited route-specific threshold sources.
- Some candidates have weak proxy gauges or no product-fresh live gauge.
- Public access and practical shuttle evidence can be thinner than in DNR water-trail states.

## Current Guidance

- Use targeted research rather than cadence.
- Prioritize corridors with official public access, direct USGS gauges, and manager/local level guidance.
- Avoid shipping route records from access-map presence alone.
